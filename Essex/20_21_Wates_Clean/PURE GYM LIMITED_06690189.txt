   Corporate governance For the year ended 31 December 2021, under the Companies (Miscellaneous Reporting) Regulations 2018 ('the Regulations'), the Company has applied the Wates Corporate Governance Principles for Large Private Companies ('Wates Principles'). A full explanation of the ways in which the Company applies the Wates Principles is included in the consolidated financial statements of the Company's parent company, Pinnacle Bidco PLC.
