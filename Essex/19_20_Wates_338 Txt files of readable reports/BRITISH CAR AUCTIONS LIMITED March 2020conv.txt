BRITISH CAR AUCTIONS LIMITED March 2020conv
Framework 
The structures, policies and procedures the Company have in place derive from the framework principles in the Wates guidance namely: purpose and leadership, Board composition, Directors' responsibilities, opportunity and risk, stakeholder relationships and engagement. BCA Limited is an integral part of the Group's operations and the corporate governance principles and direction of the Company are therefore consistent with those set out in the BBD Parentco Limited Annual Report and Accounts for the period ended 29 March 2020
