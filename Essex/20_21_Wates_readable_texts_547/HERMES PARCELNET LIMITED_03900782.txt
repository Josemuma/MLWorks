
Highlight [page 27]: Governance framework The Company is the principal trading entity within the Group and is ultimately governed by the Board of the Concert Topco Group. The Group apply the Wates corporate governance principles when considering corporate governance. The Strategic report within the Annual Report and Consolidated Financial Statements of Concert Topco Limited details the Company's and the Group's activities and considerations for each of its stakeholder groups and how the six principles of this code have been applied.
