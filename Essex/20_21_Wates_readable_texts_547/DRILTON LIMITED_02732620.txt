
Highlight [page 5]: Corporate governance The directors note that the Company is the parent undertaking of the J. Murphy & Sons Limited Group (the "Murphy Group") and consider their duties to promote the success of the Company for the benefit of its shareholders are achieved by the Murphy Group being a sustainable, profitable and cash generative business. The directors consider that the current Murphy Group strategy supports this purpose and is taken into account when they review and approve the Murphy Group annual business plan. The directors also note that the Murphy Group has adopted the Wates Corporate Governance Principles for Large Private Companies. The directors fully support this approach and further details of those corporate governance arrangements will be found in the financial statements of its subsidiary, J. Murphy & Sons Limited, for the year ended 31 December 2021.
