
Highlight [page 8]: Adoption of governance code: During the period, the Company considered the adoption of a corporate governance code. In choosing the appropriate code for the Company, the board sought to reflect the interconnection between the governance of the Company as a subsidiary of SSP Group pie and the policies and decisions taken at a Group level. The directors agreed it was appropriate for the Company to adopt its own Subsidiary Code, building upon the core principles contained within The Wates Corporate Governance Principles For Large Private Companies.
