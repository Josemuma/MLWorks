
Highlight [page 29]: Compliance with the UK corporate governance code This section of the financial statements, together with the Section 172 (1) statement in the Strategic Report on pages 8 to 10, the Sustainability Report in the Strategic Report on pages 16 to 25, the Directors' responsibilities statement on page 30 and note 11 'Key management remuneration', describes how the Group has applied the main Wates principles contained within the 2018 edition of the UK Corporate Governance Code (the 'Code'). The Group confirms that, throughout the 53 week period ended 1 January 2022, it complied with the principles and provisions of the Code. A copy of the 2018 edition of the Code can be found on the Financial Reporting Council's website at www.frc.org.uk.
